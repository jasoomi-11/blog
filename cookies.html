Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Blueverse uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on the site.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember preferences, improve performance and provide analytics.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for site functionality (navigation, security).
  • Performance & analytics: Collect anonymous usage data (page views, device type) to improve content.
  • Preference cookies: Remember language, layout and user choices.
  • Advertising: If we use third-party ads, those providers may set cookies – we do not control them.

Managing cookies

You can disable cookies from your browser settings. Note: disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services (analytics, ads). These services have their own cookie policies. Blueverse is not responsible for third-party cookie behavior.

Changes to this policy

We may update cookie usage — changes will be posted here.
