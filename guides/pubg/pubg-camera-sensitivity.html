PUBG Mobile – Game Summary

PUBG Mobile

Developed by: Tencent Games & Krafton

PUBG Mobile is a competitive battle royale game where 100 players land, loot, fight and survive. Featuring realistic guns, tactical gameplay, smooth performance, maps, vehicles and intense close-range gunfights, it's one of the most popular mobile eSports titles worldwide.

Best Camera Sensitivity (Recommended – PUBG Mobile)

These camera sensitivity settings are perfect for achieving fast aim movement, smooth screen control, and better tracking during close-range and mid-range gunfights. Designed for both beginners and experienced players who want consistent recoil control and stable camera movement in PUBG Mobile.

Advanced Camera Tips +

  • Use separate sensitivities for Gyro + ADS if you play hybrid.

If you want zero-shake aim and laser recoil control during sprays, this gyro setup is ideal for competitive players and eSports-level gameplay.

  • For full sprays, keep 3x & 4x Gyro lower than hip-fire gyro.

Best Sensitivity for Low-End, Mid-Range & Flagship Devices

Every mobile device performs differently in PUBG Mobile due to hardware limitations. These optimized sensitivity profiles ensure stable aim, low recoil, and smooth tracking across low-end, mid-range, and flagship phones. Choose your device category below to get the best possible competitive advantage.

Low-End Device Settings (2–3GB RAM) +

Low-end devices experience frame drops, so you need lower sensitivity for stability:

  • Camera Sensitivity: 90–110%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 70–90%
  • Gyroscope: 180–220%
  • 2x Scope: 70%
  • 3x Scope: 55%
  • 4x Scope: 35%
  • 6x Scope: 20%

These values reduce screen shake and help maintain recoil control even on unstable FPS.

Mid-Range Device Settings (4–6GB RAM) +

Mid-range phones can handle smoother movement so sensitivity can be higher:

  • Camera Sensitivity: 110–130%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 90–120%
  • Gyroscope: 240–300%
  • 2x Scope: 90%
  • 3x Scope: 70%
  • 4x Scope: 50%
  • 6x Scope: 30%

These settings provide accurate recoil control and fast tracking without losing stability.

Flagship Device Settings (8GB+ RAM / 90–120FPS) +

Flagships offer stable FPS, so high sensitivity gives you lightning-fast aim:

  • Camera Sensitivity: 130–160%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 120–150%
  • Gyroscope: 300–350%
  • 2x Scope: 110%
  • 3x Scope: 95%
  • 4x Scope: 70%
  • 6x Scope: 40%

Higher values allow quick flicks, precise recoil control, and pro-level movement.

Scope-by-Scope Sensitivity Breakdown

Each scope in PUBG Mobile behaves differently, so using the same sensitivity across all scopes creates recoil issues. This scope-by-scope breakdown gives you the best tracking, zero-shake ADS control, and laser recoil stability for every range of combat.

No Scope (Hip Fire) +

No scope sensitivity controls hip-fire recoil and movement tracking. Best for close-range fights.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 150–180%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 120–150%
  • Gyroscope: 250–300%

Higher values improve spray transfer in close combats.

Red Dot, Holographic & Aim Assist +

Red Dot is the most balanced scope for short and mid-range combat.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 40–55%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 35–50%
  • Gyroscope: 280–320%

Perfect for SMG and AR close combat recoil control.

2x Scope +

Best for mid-range AR sprays and SMG tracking.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 35–45%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 30–40%
  • Gyroscope: 240–280%
3x Scope +

3x is the best for long mid-range recoil spray techniques.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 30–40%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 25–35%
  • Gyroscope: 220–260%
4x Scope +

Used for long-range AR sprays and DMR stability.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 20–30%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 15–25%
  • Gyroscope: 150–200%
6x Scope (Converted 3x Spray) +

6x is mainly used for long-range converted sprays.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 10–18%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 10–15%
  • Gyroscope: 80–120%

Lower sensitivity helps reduce heavy recoil kick.

8x Scope (Sniping) +

Used only for bolt-action sniper rifles.

  • Camera Sensitivity: 8–12%
  • ADS Sensitivity: 6–10%
  • Gyroscope: 45–60%

Lower values deliver pixel-perfect sniping control.

Ultimate Spray Control Guide (Complete Breakdown)

This PUBG Mobile spray control guide helps you master close-range, mid-range and long-range AR sprays with stable recoil, zero shake, and perfect tracking. Ye guide un players ke liye perfect hai jo laser-like sprays chahte hain — chahe M416 ho, AKM ho, ya Scar-L.

1. Choose the Right Weapon & Attachments +

Sahi attachments spray control ka 70% recoil handle kar deti hain.

  • M416 – Best overall recoil + stability
  • Scar-L – Stable but slightly less accurate
  • AKM – High damage, high recoil (requires Gyro)
  • DP-28 – Zero recoil beam for long range

Best Attachments for Laser Spray:

  • Compensator
  • Vertical Foregrip
  • Tactical Stock
  • Extended Quickdraw Mag
2. Perfect Crosshair Positioning (Most Ignored Trick) +

Crosshair ko hamesha enemy ke upper chest ya neck level per rakhna chahiye.

  • Crosshair high rakhne se recoil climb par headshots milte hain
  • Aim swipe kam hota hai → control zyada smooth hota hai
  • Tracking fast ho jati hai, specially GYRO users ke liye
3. How to Pull Recoil Down Correctly +

Recoil pull ka method dono users ke liye different hota hai:

• Gyroscope Players:

  • Mobile ko halkay se downward tilt karo
  • Forceful movement recoil ko unstable banata hai
  • Jitna zyada scope zoom, utna halka tilt

• Non-Gyro Players:

  • Right thumb ko slow and steady downward drag karo
  • Spray start hone ke 0.3 sec baad pull shuru karo
  • Too much dragging = spray shake
4. Long-Range Spray (Scope 3x, 4x, 6x) +

Long-range sprays ka secret recoil control + micro tracking hota hai.

  • Always crouch before long-range spray
  • 3-4 bullets ke baad recoil control stable ho jata hai
  • 6x ko 3x me convert kar ke spray karein (much easier)
  • Target ke body center per aim rakho, recoil naturally headshots dega
5. How to Track Moving Enemies (Pro Technique) +

Moving targets ko spray karne ka sabse simple rule:

  • Enemy ki movement direction ke saath-saath swipe karo
  • Recoil pull + horizontal tracking saath hona chahiye
  • High gyro players sideways tilt use karein (best method)
  • Strafing (A-D movement) spray stability ko 30% improve karta hai
