PUBG:Mobile 90FPS Supported Phones

By Blueverse Gaming | Dec 8, 2025

Introduction – Why 90 FPS Matters in PUBG Mobile

In PUBG Mobile, higher frame rates play a massive role in overall performance and combat consistency. 90 FPS (frames per second) gives players smoother visuals, cleaner animations, and more responsive controls. As competitive gaming evolves, 90 FPS is becoming a standard requirement for serious rank pushers, competitive players, and anyone who wants a stable, fluid gameplay experience.

Smooth Gameplay

90 FPS makes the entire game feel smoother, from running and rotating to aiming and peeking. The character movements become more fluid, and you experience fewer micro-stutters. Smooth gameplay is especially important during close-range fights where every frame matters. With higher FPS, each action is displayed more accurately, giving you better control of movement and recoil handling.

Competitive Advantage

Higher FPS directly improves your competitive capability. Players with 90 FPS can see enemy movement more clearly and react faster during intense fights. Even small visual improvements—like cleaner strafe animations or smoother ADS transitions—can give a noticeable edge against players locked at 30 or 60 FPS. This advantage becomes crucial in TPP peeks, sprays, jiggle fights, and mid-range tracking.

Faster Reaction Time

When your screen refreshes more often, information reaches your eyes sooner. This means your reaction time becomes faster without any extra effort. In a game where milliseconds decide the winner of a 1v1 spray battle, 90 FPS gives you a measurable head start. Faster reaction processing also reduces input delay, allowing you to switch weapons, lean, jump, or crouch more precisely during combat.

Official 90 FPS Support in PUBG Mobile

PUBG Mobile has gradually expanded high-frame-rate support across different versions such as Global, KR, and BGMI. While the game engine was originally optimized for a 60 FPS cap, new device capabilities and competitive demand pushed developers to officially unlock 90 FPS for selected models. Understanding how 90 FPS support works, which regions received it first, and what requirements are needed helps players optimize their gameplay and device choice.

PUBG Mobile Engine FPS Limits

PUBG Mobile is based on the Unreal Engine, which defines FPS tiers like Smooth, Balanced, HD, HDR, Ultra HD, and Extreme modes. Initially, the engine limited FPS to 30 and 60 depending on graphics settings. However, as gaming phones improved and 90Hz+ displays became common, the engine was updated to support 90 FPS on Smooth graphics for specific devices.

Despite the update, 90 FPS is not universally available on every device. The game checks several compatibility factors such as GPU capability, display refresh rate, temperature stability, and region-based restrictions before allowing the option.

Global, KR, BGMI 90 FPS Timeline

Different regional versions of PUBG Mobile released 90 FPS at different times. Some regions received it earlier for testing and optimization, while others unlocked it later depending on regulations and device support lists.

Version 90 FPS Release Timeline Notes
Global Mid–2020 onwards First version to test 90 FPS on select flagship devices (OnePlus partnership period).
KR/Japan Late–2020 90 FPS became stable and more widely supported compared to Global.
BGMI (India) 2021–2022 Gradual rollout; initially locked on many devices due to local optimization limits.

Requirements for Enabling 90 FPS

Even though PUBG Mobile officially supports 90 FPS, it only appears on devices that meet certain hardware and software conditions. If any requirement is missing, the 90 FPS option remains hidden from the graphics menu.

Requirement Explanation
90Hz or higher display The device must support at least a 90Hz refresh rate (120Hz or 144Hz also supported).
Supported chipset High-end or gaming chipsets like Snapdragon 855+, 865, 888, 8 Gen series, Dimensity 8000+ etc.
Official whitelist support Device must be included in PUBG’s internal supported-device list.
Updated version of PUBG Older APK versions may not show 90 FPS even if the device supports it.
Temperature stability If a device overheats, PUBG may automatically drop FPS options for protection.

If your device meets these requirements, 90 FPS will automatically appear under the “Smooth” graphics setting. Players who want stable performance should also enable performance mode or high refresh rate mode from the device settings.

Snapdragon Devices With Full 90 FPS Support

PUBG Mobile has optimized 90 FPS for a wide range of Snapdragon-powered devices, especially those with high-performance CPUs, strong GPUs, and stable thermal systems. Snapdragon chipsets are known for their consistent performance, which is why most competitive players prefer them for smooth and stable gameplay. Below is a clear breakdown of which Snapdragon series deliver full 90 FPS support and how each category performs in real gameplay situations.

Snapdragon 8 Gen Series


The Snapdragon 8 Gen lineup is currently the strongest and most reliable option for achieving full 90 FPS in PUBG Mobile. Devices powered by Snapdragon 8 Gen 1, 8+ Gen 1, 8 Gen 2, and 8 Gen 3 consistently provide stable high-frame-rate gameplay even during long sessions. The combination of powerful Kryo cores and Adreno GPUs ensures smooth animations, fast loading, and minimal frame drops. These devices easily maintain 90 FPS on Smooth settings and are highly favored in the competitive community.

Snapdragon 7 Gen Series

Snapdragon 7 Gen chipsets offer a balanced mix of performance and efficiency. The latest models like Snapdragon 7+ Gen 2 and 7 Gen 3 are capable of delivering near-flagship results, making them suitable for 90 FPS in PUBG Mobile when optimized properly. While they may not match the thermal stability of flagship processors, they still provide smooth motion, responsive controls, and reliable mid-to-high performance. These devices are ideal for players who want 90 FPS without paying flagship prices.

Older Snapdragon Flagship Chipsets

Previous-generation flagship chipsets such as Snapdragon 855+, 865, 870, and even 888 still handle 90 FPS surprisingly well. These processors come with strong Adreno GPUs that were designed to run high-performance games, which allows them to deliver smooth framerates on PUBG Mobile even today. While thermal throttling can appear on some older devices, they remain highly capable and continue to be excellent budget-friendly choices for players who want stable 90 FPS performance.

Mid-Range Devices With Unofficial Support

Some mid-range Snapdragon devices—especially those using Snapdragon 695, 732G, 778G, or similar processors—do not always show official 90 FPS support in PUBG Mobile. However, many of these chipsets are technically capable of running higher frame rates due to strong GPU efficiency and good refresh-rate displays. In some regional versions or after specific updates, these devices may receive partial support, but stability varies from model to model. These devices can offer improved smoothness compared to standard 60 FPS, but they cannot match the consistency of high-end Snapdragon models.

MediaTek Devices That Support 90 FPS in PUBG Mobile

MediaTek ki Dimensity series ne PUBG Mobile ke andar gaming performance ko kaafi upgrade kiya hai. Aaj kal bahut se MediaTek devices official 90 FPS support karte hain, bas condition ye hoti hai ke PUBG engine un models ko allow kare. Neeche sari chips ka clear, human-written, professional breakdown diya gaya hai.

1. Dimensity 9000 / 9200 / 9300 Series

Ye MediaTek ki flagship-level chipsets hain jo direct Snapdragon 8 Gen series ko compete karti hain. PUBG Mobile me ye processors stable 90 FPS de sakte hain agar device optimized ho. Inka GPU power, thermals, aur sustained performance competitive gaming ke liye perfect hai — hot drops aur close-range fights me bhi FPS stable rehta hai.

2. Dimensity 8000 / 8200 Series

Ye mid-range price me flagship-level gaming experience dete hain. PUBG Mobile me ye chipsets kaafi smooth 90 FPS handling rakhte hain jab game officially allow karta hai. Thermal performance balanced hoti hai aur long matches me FPS drop kam hota hai. Budget players ke liye ye perfect “performance-per-rupee” choice hai.

3. Dimensity 1000+ and Similar Chipsets

Ye older flagship chips hain, lekin PUBG Mobile me phir bhi 90 FPS ka stable output de sakte hain supported devices par. Performance itni strong nahi jitni new Dimensity series me hoti hai, lekin phir bhi budget-friendly high refresh rate gaming ke liye perfect choice hain.

Conclusion – Which Devices Perform Best at 90 FPS?

When it comes to achieving smooth, stable, and truly competitive 90 FPS gameplay in PUBG Mobile, not all devices perform equally. Modern hardware has improved across all tiers, but the biggest difference comes from thermal efficiency, chipset power, and overall software optimization.

Flagship vs Mid-Range

Flagship phones clearly dominate in 90 FPS performance. Their advanced chipsets, stronger GPUs, larger vapor chambers, and optimized firmware ensure extremely stable gameplay. Mid-range phones can deliver 90 FPS in many situations, but they cannot maintain the same level of consistency during long sessions or high-heated matches.

• Flagships = Best stability, lowest touch delay, strongest cooling • Mid-range = Good performance but may struggle during hot drops and long matches

Long-Term Performance

Long-term stability heavily depends on how well a device manages heat. Powerful chipsets may deliver 90 FPS initially, but without good cooling they eventually throttle. This is where flagships shine — they maintain performance over extended gaming sessions without major frame drops.

Devices like the ROG Phone series, Samsung S24 Ultra, and iPhone Pro models offer long-session consistency, making them ideal for competitive players. Mid-range phones perform well for shorter matches but are more prone to FPS dips during intense gameplay.

Final Recommendations

If your main goal is to play PUBG Mobile at a smooth and stable 90 FPS, then choosing a flagship device is the most reliable option. They provide the best touch response, minimal lag, and highest frame stability — all of which matter during competitive fights and close-range battles.

Best choices overall:

• iPhone 15 Pro / 15 Pro Max • Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra • ASUS ROG Phone 8 / 8 Pro • Xiaomi 14 Ultra

Mid-range phones with Snapdragon 7+ Gen 2 or Dimensity 8100/8200 are excellent for their price, but serious players aiming for long-term 90 FPS performance will benefit most from premium flagships.

In short: If you want the smoothest 90 FPS experience, go flagship. If you want value with good performance, choose a strong mid-range device.
